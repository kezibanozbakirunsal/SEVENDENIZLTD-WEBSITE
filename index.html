About SEVENDENIZ COMPANY

SEVENDENIZ COMPANY is a Manchester-based interior design service specialising in fitted wardrobes, bespoke TV units, kitchen cabinets and careful mounting work. Every project is treated as a one-off: we measure, plan and install with great attention to detail, so that every millimetre works for your space.

From small flats to full family homes, we focus on clean lines, soft colours and practical storage. Our goal is to make your rooms feel lighter, calmer and more organised — without losing your personal style.

✓ Made-to-measure wardrobes and dressing rooms for awkward spaces, sloping ceilings and alcoves.
✓ TV units, media walls and feature walls that hide cables and create a tidy, modern focal point.
✓ Careful assembly of IKEA and flat-pack furniture, plus secure mounting of shelves, mirrors and TVs.
✓ Friendly, tidy and reliable service across Manchester and surrounding areas.
